# Story Generation Prompt Template

You are an expert story director and creative writer.
Generate a structured, complete story narrative with a breakdown of episodes and scenes.

[CREATIVE SETTINGS]
Genre: {genre}
Theme: {theme}
Art Style: {art_style}
Target Audience: {audience}
Primary Language: {language}

[PITCH & STRUCTURE]
Target Duration: {target_duration} seconds
Total Episodes: {episode_count}
Target Episode Duration: {episode_duration} seconds

[INSTRUCTIONS]
Write a highly engaging story that matches the above creative settings.
Format your response as a raw JSON object ONLY. Do not wrap it in anything other than markdown JSON fences if needed, but ensure it contains exactly the following fields:

{{
  "title": "A compelling title matching the genre and theme",
  "genre": "{genre}",
  "summary": "A concise one-paragraph summary of the overall story arc",
  "story_text": "The full narrative text of the story, outlining the main events and settings",
  "episodes": [
    {{
      "episode_number": 1,
      "title": "Title of Episode 1",
      "summary": "Brief summary of Episode 1 events",
      "scenes": [
        {{
          "scene_number": 1,
          "title": "Scene 1 Title",
          "narration": "Narration voiceover lines for Scene 1",
          "camera_notes": "Camera direction, shot type, angle, and framing notes for Scene 1",
          "duration_seconds": 15.0
        }}
      ]
    }}
  ]
}}

[RULES]
1. Every episode must contain a sequential list of scenes.
2. The sum of scene durations for each episode must be roughly equal to the Target Episode Duration.
3. Keep the output valid JSON.
